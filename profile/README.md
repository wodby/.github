## Wodby, Inc

Wodby is a Kubernetes Platform as a Service that helps you deploy your apps to Kubernetes without vendor lock-in.

We provide configurable stacks with auto-updates to simplify deployment to Kubernetes. All of our docker images and helm charts are public.
